New York City

It really does not get much better than New York City. You can not talk about big metropolitan cities without recognizing New York City's inspiration for past, present, and future cities!

Some interesting facts about New york City:

  • The original name of the city was New Amsterdam
  • I'm sure a lot of people know this but the Statue of Liberty was a gift from the French. Believe it or not the French can be nice sometimes.
  • The first pizzeria in the United States was opened in New York in 1905!
  • Honking your car is actually illegal, but, if you've ever been in New York City you know people tend to ignore this law.
  • New York taxis were originally red and green. They only changed to the iconic Yellow in 1912
  • There are a total of 469 subway stops in New York City, in Toronto there are 75 stations and we're a World Class City right? Right??
  • More than 800 languages are spoken in New York City, making it the most linguistically diverse city in the worls! 4 out of 10 households speak a language other than English, talk about culturally diverse!
  • New York City became the first capital of the United States in 1789, imagine what would the city be like if that was still true today!

Reasons to visit New York City:

  • It's NEW YORK CITY! You have to visit New York City at least once in your life!
  • Museums and Art Galleries: New York City has some of the most impressive artistic prowesses of the whole world, and it really shows in the wide variety of art galleries and museums offered.
  • The skyscrapers: It can't really be a World Class City without the skyscrapers, they are truly one of the pinacles of human engineering. The Empire State Building and the One World Observatory are a must visit in any New York trip.
  • Live music and Sport Events: If you are a fan of music and sports you will have an awesome time in New York. Pretty much anyday of the week you'll be sure to find sources of entertainment in New York. It is called the city that never sleeps afterall.
  • Times Square: The iconic Times Square, although not a favourite for a lot of the locals, it is a must visit for pretty much everyone else! After seeing it in a variety of different films, seeing it in person is a whole other experience.
  • FOOD! Who doesn't like food? And more so, who doesn't like to try different cuisines from all over the world! All this can be found in New York's exquisite gastronomic diversity
